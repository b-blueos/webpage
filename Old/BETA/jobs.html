Want to contribute?

We search for designers and developers! If you would like to join our crew there are a few rules:
1. Have a genuine interest in contributing, we don't want people that will just sit back and not do much.
2. You won't get any money for any help, as this is a project that is made for free. (NO ASKING FOR MONEY, basically.) And we take no donations yet.
